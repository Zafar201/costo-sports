Agency

Inspiration

Our sports agency was inspired by the voices of the ghetto people and those passionate about football. Namely, Costa Sports Pro (CSpro) was formed as a result of data from a research in 2020 in collaboration with the Johan Cruijff Institute. In this research, the majority of Zimbabweans reported that an agency specifically targeting ghetto youths is needed to improve local football in Zimbabwe.

  • our mission




    CSpro agency is a sports organisation that assists all ghetto aspiring soccer players to achieve their dreams. We educate these youths about professional football culture and through our European links we provide the bridge to cross over to competitive soccer. We link education and football, providing the youths with brighter and broader future options.

  • our VISION

      Our vision is to be able to transform talented ghetto youths into professional footballers, and to provide a learning platform for sports. CSpro also aims to facilitate the betterment of local community lifestyles through football and to improve the perception of professional soccer in the ghetto. We want to represent players that will feature in various top-flight football leagues around the world.

what we do

    • MANAGING

      • Assist local football players in directing, maintaining and improving their professional careers. In order to become part of CSpro Agency and experience our expert guide, please register

    • EDUCATORS

      • We are mediators who negotiate changes in agreements on behalf of the players. This part of the agency’s activities will negotiate, educate and advise players regarding signing of professional contracts.

    • OPPORTUNITIES

      • CSpro offers possibilities and chances for youth and talented players to switch to Europe for development.
