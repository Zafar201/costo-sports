Training

our Training

Training Schedule & Venue

Recreational teams typically trains 3 times a week plus weekends. Location and times are determined by the coach. All trainings are held after school. Youths who miss school to attend trainings sessions, will not be admitted.

Parents/guardians are permitted to watch training sessions if they wish so. Parents/guardians can also volunteer to help with training sessions and the head coach will decide about their duties.

Costa Sports Pro (CSpro) offers specialized training sessions for players with aims to play professional locally and abroad.

CSpro typically use the Unit L training ground or Seke 6 High school. In case of any changes, players and parents/guardians will be notified earlier.

Tournaments

Tournaments are optional and will be arranged by the management. These may include school tournaments as well as tournaments against other local teams. Since we have international links, such tournaments could also include international travel in the event of special invitations from hosts abroad, especially Europe. We also include schools from rural areas since we aim to provide opportunities to the ones who most need them.

CSpro youth players are coached by a qualified coach as well as by volunteer parents for the recreational teams. The local community is an important part for CSpro Academy, hence its participation is highly valued. The head coach has the final say regarding training sessions and venues. The academy seeks to provide the best possible conditions for the youths

Evaluations/Tryouts

Evaluations are not required for recreational play. Those youths who are able to attend training session with consent from their parents/guardians will be welcomed.

Players interested in participating in competitive teams/levels could be provided with separate specific trainings according to the arrangement by the management team. Apart from recreational play, CSpro also helps those players who seek to turn professional by providing them with advanced training programs such as, specified cardio training, advice on strength training and nutrition. Such specific training may include individual training drills at designated times. Training abroad is also possible given that CSpro has foreign links.

CSpro will assist any player who is into football to achieve their dreams whenever possible by providing them with the necessary tools within our reach.
